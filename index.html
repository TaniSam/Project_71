Library

The Library of this renowned school aims at developing a comprehensive collection of materials in the Science,Arts and Commerce,giving vital support to teaching in the School.The Library Operation is computerized.There is a wealth of materials which includes Books,Journals, Dictionary,Fictions, Encyclopedias & General Reference Also.The Library incorporated newer dimensions helping students in acquiring excellence in their curricular and co-curricular pursuits of education.

Computer Lab

The computer lab is my favourite lab (I haven't gone to any other lab due to lockdown) It is the place where we do the things we learnt, in practical. It has more than 95 computers with a projector and several air conditioners.

Service Learning Projects

The School gives a lot of emphasis on service with love for the needy and less privileged groups of the society. The students are taught to appreciate the blessings bestowed upon them by God and share whatever they can to help the poor and the deprived. For this end we conduct the Social Service Week every year to collect certain basic items such as rice, cereals, clothes, biscuits, soaps, books and stationery and cash. The children are taken to various locations to practice what they learn. The students distribute the collected items to the needy during their field trips.

Godric Gryffindor

Godric Gryffindor

The founder of Gryffindor house. A fair man, he believed that any child who displayed magical abilities before their 11th birthday should be able to attend Hogwarts. Godric valued courage and bravery, believing these were the two most virtuous abilities a person can possess. Gryffindor was the original owner of the Sorting Hat, which he and his fellow founders enchanted to select people to be sorted into their houses, by seeing the qualities each founder valued the most. His house corresponds to the element of fire.

Helga Hufflepuff

Helga Hufflepuf

The founder of Hufflepuff house. A kind and warm woman, she believed that, in order to be sorted into Hufflepuff house, a person must possess the values of loyalty, patience, and be hard-working above all else. It is also known that she brought the house-elves to Hogwarts. She owned a fine golden cup which was fairly innocuous aside from being her possession. The cup was sought by Lord Voldemort nearly a thousand years later, for him to turn it into a Horcrux. She was the best friend of Rowena Ravenclaw. Her house corresponds to the element of earth.
